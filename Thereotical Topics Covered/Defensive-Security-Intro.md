# Defensive Security Introduction

## Overview
This room focused on defensive cybersecurity practices and the role of blue teams in protecting systems and networks.

- Security Operations Center (SOC)
- Log monitoring and analysis
- Threat detection
- Incident response basics

## Key Takeaways
- Understanding the role of SOC analysts.
- Understanding the role of SOC Engineer.
- Importance of monitoring logs and alerts.
- Basic workflow of detecting and responding to threats.
