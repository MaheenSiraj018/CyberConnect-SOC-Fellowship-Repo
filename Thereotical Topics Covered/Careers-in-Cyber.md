# Careers in Cyber

## Overview
This room explored various career paths within cybersecurity and the skills required for each role.

## Topics Covered
- SOC Analyst
- Penetration Tester
- Threat Hunter
- Security Engineer
- Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)

## Key Takeaways
- Differences between blue team and red team careers.
- Importance of continuous learning and certifications.
- Understanding potential career progression paths in cybersecurity.
